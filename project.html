Key Energy Audit Projects

We have successfully conducted energy audits for a wide range of businesses, ensuring compliance with the RA 11285 Energy Efficiency & Conservation (EE&C) Act.

  • Liberty Commercial Center (LCC): Multiple expressmart and supermarket locations
  • Lyceum of the Philippines University - Batangas: Main, Riverside, and Lima Campuses
  • Felton Properties: Mercedes-Benz, Jeep, Land Rover, Jaguar, Harley Davidson Showrooms
  • The Palms Country Club: Energy audits and energy management services
  • Residential Buildings: Compliance assessments for major condominium complexes

Other Completed Projects

Aside from energy audits, we have completed numerous projects related to energy optimization, system upgrades, and regulatory compliance.

Project Success Metrics
